LDEO VPN Installation

A VPN is a virtual private network. If you are off-campus, you can log on to the LDEO VPN and your computer's IP address will make it look like you are also physically on campus. This is useful for accessing online material than is only availble from on campus.

Point your web browser to the LDEO VPN log in screen

https://vpn.ldeo.columbia.edu

Click on the drop down list and choose "Login".

Log in with your LDEO ID and Password.

After you have successfully logged in you will be able to download and install the new "OpenVPN Connect" Client and download the configuration file.

On OS X, this will put the OpenVPN login tool up on the menu bar.
